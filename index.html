Carbon-neutral investments

Venox in partnership with MOSS offsets carbon produced by digital assets in our Origin portfolios.

A portion of the portfolio is converted into the Moss Carbon Credit (MCO2) token which represents tons of carbon. Your investment will be used to save the Amazon with the worlds first green digital asset backed by an official agent at the largest and most respected global voluntary carbon registry. Carbon credits are predicted to appreciate in price substantially as more investment is required in projects that take carbon out of the atmosphere in the long-term.

Learn more about Moss and its projects at https://moss.earth
